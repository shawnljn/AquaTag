# Electronics design

Planned contents:

- Editable schematic and PCB layout sources
- PDF schematic
- Bill of materials with manufacturer part numbers and approved substitutions
- Gerber, drill, centroid/position, and assembly drawings
- Pick-and-place and PCBA ordering notes
- Programming/debug connector pinout and test points
- Power budget and interface map

The paper prototype is centered on an nRF52832 MCU, BMI270 IMU, MS5540 pressure/temperature sensor, W25Q128 NOR flash, optional microSD storage, BQ25180 battery management, TPS71818-33 regulation, and a CX-4mm-JZ magnetic charging connector.

When uploading the design, record the EDA application and version, PCB stack-up, board thickness, copper weight, finish, assembly options, and the exact revision tested in the paper.
